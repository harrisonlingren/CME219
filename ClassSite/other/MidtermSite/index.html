Be a part of the future. Butler's Interactive Media program immerses students in the design and development of web technologies that people use every day on computers, tablets, and mobile devices.

As an Interactive Media major, you will:

  • Learn front-end and back-end development of interactive web-content.
  • Develop, test, and complete a variety of usable projects.
  • Design appealing visual elements.
  • Master HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and other programming languages.

A degree in Interactive Media will prepare students to find employment in a variety of job markets. With the expansion of e-commerce, Web design and development professionals are in high demand and students from this degree program will have a highly sought educational background in both design and development, putting them agead of their competition.
