Interactive Media Requirements

Students interested in a minor in Interactive Media will take a total of 12 credit hours made up of the following required courses:

Course No. Course Title Credit Hours
Required Courses
CS142 Computer Science and Programming 3
CME208 Multimedia Graphics 3
CME219 Design for the Web 3
CME308 Interactive Design 3
3 Courses (2 300-level or higher) from these courses:
CME106 Survey of Digital Media 3
MK280 Principles of Marketing 3
CME306 Multimedia Design and Production 3
CME419 Interactive Web Development 3
CME422 New Media Distribution 3
CME432 Law & Regulation of Electronic Media 3

Current Students should consult their own academic advisement report in my.butler.edu to see their individual requirements and progress toward degree completion.
