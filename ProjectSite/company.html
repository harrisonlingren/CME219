Welcome to Tony's Pizzeria!

Tony’s Pizzeria was first established in 1905 in Indianapolis, Indiana. Located in the Broadripple area close to Butler University, Tony’s Pizzeria quickly became a popular hangout for college students and locals. Due to its success among the college crowd, Tony’s soon opened locations in and around the Indianapolis area.

Even with several locations, Tony’s Pizzeria still remains family run. Tony’s family immigrated from Italy and its these authentic Italian recipes that make their pizzas one-of-a-kind. Using only fresh ingredients, Tony makes his own dough, sauce, and homemade mozzarella daily. Its no wonder Tony’s Pizzeria has been voted the Best Pizza in Indy several times over!
